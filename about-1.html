About Us

Mensa DigiWorld is a ground-breaking, imaginative and responsive organization geared and dedicated to the provision of high-quality web and software solutions for the real IT world and advance them in it. We want to take Mensa DigiWorld to the next level. Our History

From the inception of Mensa Digiworld in 2016, it has been always focused on the customized IT solution for specific requirements. As the days went by, with every new customer Mensa was creating a significant portfolio of various types of solutions with both local and foreign client base. Within 4 years of presence in the industry Mensa created 200 individual management system software as response to the customer’s specific requirements.

Our Mission

Our mission statement is as simple as it can be read but is very thought of and planned process to make any kind of business an easy affair. The company will provide integrated software solutions for businesses ranging from small to medium to large in numerous fields. The mission is to make businesses easy by enabling them with not just latest but the most suitable and highly customized software solutions to the clients. The solutions will upgrade the processes as well leverage the business by making it more manageable
